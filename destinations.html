All Destinations

  • Paris, France

    Paris, the City of Love, is famous for its iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and Notre-Dame Cathedral. Stroll along the Seine River, enjoy world-class cuisine, and immerse yourself in art and history.

    • Eiffel Tower
    • Louvre Museum
    • Notre-Dame Cathedral
    • Seine River Cruise

  • Tokyo, Japan

    Tokyo is a bustling metropolis where tradition meets modernity. Explore ancient temples, futuristic skyscrapers, and vibrant neighborhoods like Shibuya and Shinjuku. Don’t miss the cherry blossoms in spring!

    • Shibuya Crossing
    • Meiji Shrine
    • Tokyo Skytree
    • Tsukiji Fish Market

  • New York, USA

    New York City, the Big Apple, is a global hub of culture, finance, and entertainment. Visit Times Square, Central Park, the Statue of Liberty, and Broadway for an unforgettable experience.

    • Statue of Liberty
    • Central Park
    • Times Square
    • Broadway Shows

  • Bali, Indonesia

    Bali is a tropical paradise known for its stunning beaches, lush rice terraces, and vibrant culture. Relax in Ubud, surf in Kuta, and explore ancient temples like Uluwatu and Tanah Lot.

    • Ubud Monkey Forest
    • Uluwatu Temple
    • Kuta Beach
    • Tegalalang Rice Terraces

  • Cape Town, South Africa

    Cape Town is a vibrant city surrounded by natural beauty. Visit Table Mountain, explore the Cape Winelands, and take a trip to Robben Island for a dose of history.

    • Table Mountain
    • Cape Winelands
    • Robben Island
    • V&A Waterfront

  • Sydney, Australia

    Sydney is Australia’s largest city, known for its stunning harbor, iconic Opera House, and beautiful beaches like Bondi and Manly. Explore the Royal Botanic Garden and climb the Harbour Bridge for breathtaking views.

    • Sydney Opera House
    • Bondi Beach
    • Harbour Bridge
    • Royal Botanic Garden
